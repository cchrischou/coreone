RIP AND TEAR BUT YOUR BROWNIES NEVER TURN OUT SQUARE AND WHEN THEY'RE STUCK YOU'RE TOTALLY OUT OF LUCK
NEED A HAND NOW THERE'S BROWNIE PAN!

Growing up, As Seen On TV commercials was something that I always looked forward to after school while watching TV. For however long, I used to rewwatch this old brownie pan commercial on YouTube due to the fascination of how perfectly square these brownies were. The commercial made them look so delicious with the colorful candies and the exciting children. I remember I used to beg my Mom to buy this pan.

Looking back now, the aesthetic of the commercial having been so 2000s coded and the usage of vibrant blue, yellows, and reds to show the urgency of the "low" price was always so captivating to me.
